    For starters, our suggestion is beetroot hummus, which can be served with raw vegetables as a snack, as a pâté to spread on toast or as a dressing for a salad. Beetroot is a versatile root that can be cooked in a variety of ways, and its consumption brings several benefits , such as promoting intestinal health and providing the body with fiber . Ingredients 200g cooked chickpeas 1 cooked beetroot 1 clove of garlic 1 tablespoon olive oil 1 dessert spoon of tahini Water, as required Black pepper, to taste Whole salt, to taste Preparation >In a food processor, combine all the ingredients. >Crush until it turns into a paste. >Adjust the seasoning. >Transfer to a container. >Serve immediately or store in the refrigerator for 2 to 3 days. Chicken Skewers Chicken, among other benefits , is an excellent source of protein, helping to increase bone density, which helps prevent diseases such as osteoporosis. Ingredients 1 tsp garlic powder 1 tsp curry powder 1 tsp turmeric powder 10 tbsp olive oil 800 g chicken breast salt to taste ½ pineapple Preparation > In a bowl, place the garlic powder, curry and turmeric. Add 3 tablespoons of olive oil and stir. > Coat the diced chicken in the mixture and season with salt. > Cut the pineapple into pieces. > Wet the skewers in water and place chicken cubes and pineapple pieces on each one alternately. > Grill the skewers for about 4 minutes on each side. > Serve the kebabs with a salad.
